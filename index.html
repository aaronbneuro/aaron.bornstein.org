Lab

    I've started a lab in the department of Cognitive Sciences at the University of California, Irvine!
     
    The lab's ongoing research investigates the influence of memories on behavior. Specific projects in progress examine the roles of episodic and working memory in decisions for reward, perceptual inference, addiction, and intertemporal choice, using computational model-driven analysis of behavior and brain activity (via fMRI, but with plans for EEG and other methods).
     
    If you're interested in working in the lab, please send me a brief (~2 paragraph) message describing your research experience and interests. Include a CV and email addresses of up to three references.
     
      Postdoc candidates: Send the above information, and include two or three relevant manuscripts (preprints are ok).
       
      PhD applicants: Applications for the 2022 PhD cohort are available beginning in September, 2021. If you would like to inquire about applying for admission in 2022, please send the above information (plus manuscripts, if any).
